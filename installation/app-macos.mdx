---
title: "macOS"
description: "Install Kisuke on macOS"
---

The current version of Kisuke requires macOS 12 (Monterey) or later.

1. [Download Kisuke](https://beta.kisuke.dev/downloads/) for macOS.
2. Open the `.dmg` and drag **Kisuke** into your Applications folder.
3. Open Kisuke from Applications and sign in with your account.

<Warning>
Looking to connect your Mac to Kisuke remotely? You'll also need [Kisuke Connect](/installation/kisuke-connect).
</Warning>
