## System requirements

The current version of Kisuke requires **macOS 12 (Monterey) or later**.

## Installation

### Step 1: Download the installer

Download the latest `.dmg` installer from [beta.kisuke.dev/downloads](https://beta.kisuke.dev/downloads/). The installer is a universal binary that works on both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.

### Step 2: Install

Open the `.dmg` file and drag **Kisuke** into your Applications folder.

### Step 3: Open Kisuke

Open Kisuke from Applications. If macOS shows a security prompt saying the app can't be opened, go to **System Settings** > **Privacy & Security** and click **Open Anyway**.

### Step 4: Sign in

Sign in with **Google** or **GitHub**. This creates your Kisuke account and links your device. Use the same account on all your devices so they can find each other automatically.

## Using AI chat

To use the built-in AI chat, sign in separately with your AI provider:

1. Open **Settings** in Kisuke
2. Under **AI Providers**, select the provider you want to use (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, etc.)
3. Sign in with your existing account for that provider

## Uninstall

1. Quit Kisuke if it's running
2. Drag **Kisuke** from Applications to the Trash
3. Empty the Trash

## Next steps

To access this Mac remotely from another device, install [Kisuke Connect on macOS](/installation/mac) on this machine.
