---
title: "Linux"
description: "Install Kisuke on Linux"
---

1. [Download Kisuke](https://beta.kisuke.dev/downloads/) for Linux.
2. Install the package for your distribution (`.deb` or `.rpm`).
3. Open Kisuke and sign in with your account.

<Tip>
Looking to connect your Linux machine to Kisuke remotely? You'll also need [Kisuke Connect](/installation/kisuke-connect).
</Tip>
