## System requirements

The current version of Kisuke requires **Windows 10 (version 1903) or later**.

## Installation

### Step 1: Download the installer

Download the latest `.exe` installer from [beta.kisuke.dev/downloads](https://beta.kisuke.dev/downloads/).

### Step 2: Run the installer

Execute the installer and follow the on-screen prompts. After installation completes, Kisuke will be available from the Start menu.

### Step 3: Open Kisuke

Launch Kisuke from the Start menu or desktop shortcut.

### Step 4: Sign in

Sign in with **Google** or **GitHub**. This creates your Kisuke account and links your device. Use the same account on all your devices so they can find each other automatically.

## Using AI chat

To use the built-in AI chat, sign in separately with your AI provider:

1. Open **Settings** in Kisuke
2. Under **AI Providers**, select the provider you want to use (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, etc.)
3. Sign in with your existing account for that provider

## Uninstall

1. Open **Settings** > **Apps** > **Installed apps**
2. Find **Kisuke**, click the three-dot menu, and select **Uninstall**

## Next steps

To access this PC remotely from another device, install [Kisuke Connect on Windows](/installation/windows) on this machine.
