---
title: "Windows"
description: "Install Kisuke on Windows"
---

The current version of Kisuke requires Windows 10 (1903) or later.

1. [Download Kisuke](https://beta.kisuke.dev/downloads/) for Windows.
2. Run the installer.
3. Open Kisuke and sign in with your account.

<Tip>
Looking to connect your PC to Kisuke remotely? You'll also need [Kisuke Connect](/installation/kisuke-connect).
</Tip>
