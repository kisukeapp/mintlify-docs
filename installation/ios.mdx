---
title: "iOS"
description: "Install Kisuke on iOS"
---

The current version of Kisuke requires iOS 15.0 or later.

1. Download Kisuke from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/kisuke).
2. Open the app and sign in with your account.
3. Grant permissions when prompted.

<Warning>
Looking to connect your iPhone to Kisuke remotely? You'll also need [Kisuke Connect](/installation/kisuke-connect).
</Warning>
