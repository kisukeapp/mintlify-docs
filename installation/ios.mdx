## System requirements

The current version of Kisuke requires **iOS 15.0 or later** (iPhone or iPad).

## Installation

### Step 1: Download from the App Store

Download Kisuke from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/kisuke). Alternatively, visit [beta.kisuke.dev/downloads](https://beta.kisuke.dev/downloads/) from your device and tap the iOS download link.

### Step 2: Open Kisuke

Open the app from your home screen. On first launch, you'll be presented with the sign-in screen.

### Step 3: Sign in

Sign in with **Google** or **GitHub**. This creates your Kisuke account and links your device. Use the same account on all your devices so they can find each other automatically.

### Step 4: Grant permissions

When prompted, grant the requested permissions. These allow Kisuke to function properly in the background and communicate with your other devices.

## Using AI chat

To use the built-in AI chat, sign in separately with your AI provider:

1. Open **Settings** in Kisuke
2. Under **AI Providers**, tap the provider you want to use (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, etc.)
3. Sign in with your existing account for that provider

## Next steps

To access your desktop or server remotely from your phone, install [Kisuke Connect](/installation/kisuke-connect) on the machine you want to connect to.
