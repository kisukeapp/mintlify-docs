## Requirements

### iOS
- iOS 15.0 or later
- iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch

### Android
- Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later
- ARM64 or x86_64 device

## First Launch

1. Open Kisuke after installation
2. Choose your sign-in method:
   - **Claude** - Use your Anthropic account
   - **Google** - Use your Google account
   - **OpenAI** - Use your OpenAI account
3. Grant necessary permissions when prompted
