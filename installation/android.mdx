---
title: "Android"
description: "Install Kisuke on Android"
---

The current version of Kisuke requires Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later.

1. Download Kisuke from [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=app.kisuke).
2. Open the app and sign in with your account.
3. Grant permissions when prompted.

<Tip>
Looking to connect your device to Kisuke remotely? You'll also need [Kisuke Connect](/installation/kisuke-connect).
</Tip>
