## Overview

Kisuke consists of two components:

1. **Kisuke Mobile App** — The main app for iOS and Android where you code, chat with AI, and access terminals
2. **Kisuke Connect** — A companion app for your computer or server that enables secure connections from your mobile device

## Quick Start

### Step 1: Install on Mobile

Download Kisuke from your app store:

- [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/kisuke) (iOS)
- [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=app.kisuke) (Android)

### Step 2: Install on Your Computer

Install Kisuke Connect on your development machine:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Mac">
    ```bash
    brew install kisuke-connect
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Windows">
    ```powershell
    winget install Kisuke.KisukeConnect
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Linux">
    ```bash
    curl -fsSL https://kisuke.app/install.sh | sh
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Step 3: Sign In

Sign in with the **same account** on both devices:

1. Open Kisuke on your phone and sign in
2. Open Kisuke Connect on your computer and sign in
3. Your devices will automatically discover each other

### Step 4: Connect

In Kisuke mobile:

1. Go to **Spaces** or **Terminal**
2. Select your computer from the device list
3. Start coding!
