<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS">
    Requires iOS 15.0 or later.

    Kisuke for iOS is currently available via TestFlight for invited users.

    1. Accept your TestFlight invitation email from Apple.
    2. Install [TestFlight](https://apps.apple.com/app/testflight/id899247664) from the App Store if you haven't already.
    3. Open the invitation link to install Kisuke.
    4. Open the app and sign in with your account.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android">
    Requires Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later.

    1. Download Kisuke from [Google Play](https://beta2.kisuke.dev/downloads/).
    2. Open the app and sign in with your account.
    3. Grant permissions when prompted.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="macOS">
    Requires macOS 12 (Monterey) or later.

    1. [Download Kisuke](https://beta2.kisuke.dev/downloads/) for Mac.
    2. Open Kisuke from Applications and sign in with your account.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Windows">
    Requires Windows 10 (1903) or later.

    1. [Download Kisuke](https://beta2.kisuke.dev/downloads/) for Windows.
    2. Run the installer and sign in with your account.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Linux">
    1. [Download Kisuke](https://beta2.kisuke.dev/downloads/) for Linux.
    2. Sign in with your account.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Web">
    No install needed — open Kisuke directly in your browser.

    [Open web app](https://web.kisuke.app)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
