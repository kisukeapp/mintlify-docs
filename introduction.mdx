## What is Kisuke?

Kisuke transforms your mobile device into a portable development environment. With built-in AI chat, terminal, and code editor, you can code from anywhere—on the train, at a coffee shop, or from your couch.

## How Kisuke Connect Works

Kisuke Connect creates a secure, encrypted mesh network between your devices. Once you sign in on your phone and computer with the same account:

1. **Install Kisuke** on [iOS](https://download.kisuke.app/ios), [Android](https://download.kisuke.app/android), or desktop ([Mac](https://download.kisuke.app/mac), [Windows](https://download.kisuke.app/windows), or [Linux](https://download.kisuke.app/linux))
2. **Sign in** with Google or GitHub on the Kisuke app
3. **Install & sign in** to [Kisuke Connect](/concepts/kisuke-connect) daemon or [CLI](/installation/cli-docker) on your desktop, server, or toaster — then sign in with the **same** account
4. **Connected** — use the [terminal](/features/terminal), [editor](/features/editor), and [browser](/features/browser) from anywhere

No port forwarding. No firewall rules. No VPN configuration. Just sign in and go.

## Sign In Options

Kisuke uses **Google** or **GitHub** for account sign-in and device linking.

To use AI chat, sign in separately with your AI provider:

- **Anthropic** - For Claude
- **Google** - For Gemini
- **OpenAI** - For GPT
