The Kisuke Connect desktop app is the easiest way to bridge your computer to Kisuke. It runs quietly in the background and keeps your machine connected and accessible from any device.

## Features

- **System tray / menu bar** — Kisuke Connect lives in your system tray (Windows/Linux) or menu bar (macOS). Right-click the icon to check status, view connected devices, or quit.
- **Auto-start** — Kisuke Connect starts automatically when you log in to your computer, so your machine is always reachable.
- **Sign in with browser** — Authenticate with Google or GitHub directly from the app window on first launch.
- **Automatic updates** — The app checks for updates and prompts you when a new version is available.
- **Connection status** — See at a glance whether your machine is connected and reachable from your other devices.

## Supported platforms

| Platform | Requirement |
| --- | --- |
| macOS | macOS 12 (Monterey) or later |
| Windows | Windows 10 (1903) or later |
| Linux | Kernel 4.19 or later — AppImage, `.deb`, `.rpm` |
